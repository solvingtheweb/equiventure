ITEMS REQUIRED TO UNDERWRITE FINANCING FOR A

COMMERCIAL CO-OP OR CONDOMINIUM UNIT

 

 

 

1.       COMPLETE, DETAILED PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION INCLUDING  SQUARE FOOTAGE

 

2.       IF ACQUISITION,  COPY OF FULLY EXECUTED CONTRACT OF SALE

 

3.       IF REFI, SUMMARY OF CURRENT FINANCING  including price originally paid for property, date of purchase, name, term, amortization and prepayment penalty (if applicable)

 

4.       IF THERE WILL BE SIGNIFICANT RENOVATION OR CONSTRUCTION, summary of the process, permits needed, budget, time frame, improvements to be made and work to be performed

 

5.       COOPERATIVE OR CONDOMINIUM BUILDING DETAILS including number of units, number of owner-occupied units, number of floors

 

6.       PHOTOS OF UNIT

 

7.       OWNER-OCCUPIED OR SUBLET?

If sublet, provide a summary of all leases showing escalations and expirations.

 

If owner-occupied, history/description of business occupying space, and bio/resume of principal(s)

 

Corporate financial statement, last two (2) years’ profit and loss statement for business(es), last two(2) years’ 1040 tax returns for Principal(s) owning 10% or more.

       

 

 8.     LAST TWO (2) YEARS’ FINANCIAL STATEMENTS for the co-op corporation or condo association.

 

9.        PERMISSION TO OBTAIN CREDIT REPORT(S)  See form on website.  (Please make copies for each individual in a corporation or partnership)
